CrownRush

A 3D game made with Unity. It's a barely playable RTS with a "capture the flag" twist to it.

Build your army with the gold you're given (you have to put units on the battlefield and then click the start button), and triumph by bringing the Crown back to the starting zone.

Left click to select your troops, right click to command them, space bar to use the signature ability of a selected unit

Connect4 with AIs

A command lign application that allows you to train a home made neural network to play Connect 4.

How to test quickly : Create Joueur Joueur, Create Reseau Reseau, Create MonteCarlo MC, Train Reseau MonteCarlo 100, Play Joueur Reseau. This will let you play against a neural network which had 100 games of training against a Monte carlo algorithm

The neural network can see 1 move in the future which overrides it's decisions, the rest of it's strategy is pure Overfitted non-linear mess

The architecture is a simple forward propagation model with a gradient descent
